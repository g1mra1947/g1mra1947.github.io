Events

G1MRA organises a public Spring meeting and an Autumn AGM and exhibition each year. We also attend other public shows by invitation, often with one of our portable tracks.

G1MRA groups also arrange numerous meetings, many of which are in private gardens. This is a public website, so we do not publish names or addresses for these events here. To find out about activities in your area, locate your nearest group from this list and then email the group contact quoting your membership number. You can also email the G1MRA committee officer for groups as groups@g1mra.com
